4 of my Favorite Art Supplies

  1. Graphite Pencils

    Essential for sketching and shading, they offer precision and control for both detailed and rough drawings.

  2. Alcohol Markers

    Blend smoothly, dry fast, and layer well, making them great for vibrant illustrations and comic-style art.

  3. Watercolor Paints

    Create soft, flowing effects and beautiful gradients, perfect for expressive atmospheric artwork.

  4. Fine Liner Pens

    Ideal for outlining and adding many details, they provide crisp, smudge-proof lines for ink and design work.
